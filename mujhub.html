Project Link

Hostlers face trouble checking the mess menu daily and deciding whether to eat at mess or not. Also, there are a dozen of shops with hundreds of cuisines which are not listed at one place. MUJ HUB is a WebApp which can be installed on any device, with which anyone can check the mess menu and search among all eateries with listing of their items and contact information. Aim here is to empower each hostler by not only providing the menu and contact information but also let them search and compare the items with the pricing and satisfy their appetite.
